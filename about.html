Hello!

Nice to meet you. My name is Brendan Ranney and I wanted to take an opportunity to tell you more about me and my professional journey. I begn my career as an Account Coordinator working in a support role on a larger account management team. My primary responsibilities included preparing client-facing documents and small-scale SEO activities like link management and keyword research, and also performing client-approved website changes in a custom CMS. From this initial role, I quickly moved up to be an Account Manager where I maintained a large portfolio of high-value brand clients. I held other roles at this company, including Onboarding Project Manager, Senior Manager, and Senior Account Manager. I held many responsibilities during this time including client relations, SEM expertise, management of a team of 8+, and exemplary communication skills.
The more time I spent in the digital marketing landscape, I became more and more interested in the work 'behind the curtain' with both web design and development, and SEM strategy. I researched ways to transition into this field, and in late 2023 I made the move, and began a Udemy course which would teach me how to become a full stack web developer. Through this course I've developed a passion for coding as I lose myself in the work, and I'm excited to begin my career as a developer.
In my personal life I'm more than just a developer. I'm primarily a husband to a loving wife who supported me every step of the way, and a devoted dog dad to a Siberian Husky who is fighting for my attention as I write this biography. I'm a loyal football fan to the Jacksonville Jaguars and my alma mater, Florida State University, and an avid gamer.
